About

I love AppFigures and I've been using their service for about 2 years now. In that two years I've tried all the app that integrate, but I've never found a client app that I've enjoyed using. I eventually gave up and made something crude that was not worthy of release.

So over the last Christmas break I decided to revive that old project and hack it until it became Sales.

Sales is designed to do two things very easily - keep up to date with your sales reports and your app's reviews - while on the go.

It's a functional first version with a nice clean design, and I've got many more features planned, with a public todo list on trello.
